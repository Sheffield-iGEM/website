Welcome

 

iGEM combines biology, engineering, and entrepreneurship to contribute to the field of synthetic biology and tackle real world problems in areas like medicine, environment, and manufacturing.

Each year, the competition brings together more than 6,000 University participants from across the globe from about 112 countries. This annual event is called the Giant Jamboree, the synthetic biology industry’s largest innovation event hosted by the iGEM Foundation.

Sheffield iGEM is a student-led organisation that was established in late 2015. We set up and run teams to represent the University of Sheffield at the iGEM competition.
